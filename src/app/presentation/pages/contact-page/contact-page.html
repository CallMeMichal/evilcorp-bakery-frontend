Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you take custom orders?

Yes! We love creating custom cakes and pastries for special occasions. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We provide catering for events of all sizes. Contact us to discuss your needs and get a quote.

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer a selection of gluten-free breads, cakes, and pastries. Please ask our staff for today's available options.

Can I place orders online?

Currently, we accept orders via phone or in-person. We're working on an online ordering system coming soon!
